About Our Technology

Currently, the risk of network information insecurity is increasing rapidly in number and level of danger. The methods mostly used by hackers today is to attack end-to-end technology and exploit human vulnerabilities. These techniques include social engineering, phishing, pharming, etc.

One of the steps in conducting these attacks is to deceive users with malicious Uniform Resource Locators (URLs). As a result, malicious URL detection is of great interest nowadays.

Our system employs advanced machine learning techniques based on our proprietary URL behavior analysis and attribute extraction. Moreover, bigdata technology is also exploited to improve the capability of detection based on abnormal behaviors.
